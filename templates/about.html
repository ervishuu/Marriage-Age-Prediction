People have different views when it comes to age difference between husband and wife. For instance, some people are of the opinion that a man who is way older than his wife will earn more respect from her, while some will consider the idea as irrelevant.

The ideal age gap

It’s said age is just a number but when it comes to the matters of love and relationship, the age difference between a couple can actually impact their married life. Yes, you read it right! A recent study has linked the age difference between a couple with their chances of getting a divorce. In case, you are looking for a prospective life partner, this research might be of some help

The study has an answer

A study from Emory University in Atlanta indicated the ideal age difference for a marriage to last and this data, collected after evaluating 3,000 participants, also revealed some interesting findings on divorce, kids and relationships.

Couples with five to ten years of age gap

Couples with five years of age gap were 18 per cent more likely to get divorced than the ones with a one-year age gap, which had just 3 per cent chances of calling it quits. The probability of having a divorce increases to 39 per cent for couples having a 10 year age difference.
